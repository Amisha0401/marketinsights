<h1>Milk Kefir Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2030</h1><br/><p>Milk kefir (also spelled as kephir or kefier is a fermented milk drink similar to a thin yogurt or ayran that is made from kefir grains, a specific type of mesophilic symbiotic culture.
