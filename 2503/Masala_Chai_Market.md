<h1>Masala Chai Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2030</h1><br/><p>Masala chai is an Indian tea beverage made by boiling black tea in milk and water with a mixture of aromatic herbs and spices. The term chai originated from the Hindi word chai, which was derived from the Chinese word for tea, cha. In English, this spiced tea is commonly referred to as masala chai, or simply chai, even though the term refers to tea in general in the original language. According to stories, it originates from modern India and said that the king created masala chai as an ayurvedic energizing and refreshing beverage. Numerous coffee houses use the term chai latte or chai tea latte for their version to indicate that it is made with steamed milk, much like that used to make a caff latte, but mixed with a spiced tea concentrate instead of espresso. By 1994, the term had gained currency on the U.S. coffeehouse scene.The beverage has gained worldwide popularity, becoming a feature in many coffee and tea houses.</p>
