<h1>Food Grade Phosphates Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029</h1><br/><p>Food-grade phosphates are used in the production of foods to function as buffers, sequestrants, acidulants, bases, flavors, cryoprotectants, gel accelerants, dispersants, nutrients, precipitants, and as free-flow (anticaking) or ionexchange agents.
