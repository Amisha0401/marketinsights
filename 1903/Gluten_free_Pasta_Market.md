 <p>Gluten is a protein found in wheat, rye, barley or any of their crossbred varieties and derivatives. In baking, gluten is the binding agent within the flour that prevents the baked good from crumbling. As such, gluten is found in many processed and packaged product. A gluten free product does not contain any gluten or grains such as spelt-wheat, which is gluten-rich.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>A gluten-free diet is a completely gluten-free food. Eating a gluten-free diet means strictly removing foods containing wheat bran, such as pasta, pizza, beer, oatmeal, toast, sandwiches, etc., and even delicate foods such as sauces, cakes, breads, biscuits and cakes.</p>
